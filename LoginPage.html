St. Mary's Women's Engineering College was established in year of 2008 and is located in an eco-friendly environment. and is just 3 Kms away from Guntur, a new born capital of Andhra Pradesh. We offer different courses like B.TECH (CSE & ECE),M.Tech(CSE & VLSI),MBA & Diploma for students coming from different areas of Guntur.
