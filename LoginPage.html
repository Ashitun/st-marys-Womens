ST.Mary's enginnering college

Established in 2008, St. Mary's Womens Engineering College (SMWEC), Guntur is a leading Private Un-Aided college based in Guntur.The college is popularly known as SMWEC Guntur among the students.The institute offers a wide array of academic courses and programmes that are approved by AICTE. The college, affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University, Kakinada , is widely recognized for its academic standards. St. Mary's Womens Engineering College (SMWEC), Guntur takes a holistic view of education and focuses on both, academic as well as personality development of the students.
